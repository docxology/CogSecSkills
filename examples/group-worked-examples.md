# Group Worked Expected Answers

These examples are short, defensive, and evidence-labeled. They are source-owned
reader examples, not claims that an external runtime produced them.

## Structured Analytic Techniques

Skill: `sat.analysis_of_competing_hypotheses`.

Evidence: supplied incident observations are entered into a hypothesis matrix.
Inference: the provisional ranking follows diagnostic evidence rather than
analyst preference. Gaps: missing observations and weak indicators stay visible.
Confidence: medium until indicators discriminate between alternatives.
Uncertainty: competing hypotheses remain live until new evidence changes the
matrix.

## Cognitive Security

Skill: `cognitive_security.narrative_threat_assessment`.

Evidence: public claims, provenance clues, reach signals, and manipulation
markers are listed separately. Inference: narrative threat is described only
where those observations support it. Gaps: missing origin data and audience
context are named. Confidence: bounded by source quality. Uncertainty:
alternative narrative explanations remain explicit.

## Critical Review

Skill: `critical_review.project_critical_review`.

Evidence: each finding cites a file, command result, or project artifact.
Inference: severity and recommendation follow from the cited support. Gaps:
untested paths and missing reviewer context are called out. Confidence: high
only for reproducible findings. Uncertainty: remediation remains provisional
until verified.

## OSINT Integrity

Skill: `osint_integrity.claim_provenance_verification`.

Evidence: source links or supplied excerpts are ordered into a provenance chain.
Inference: the verdict states what the chain supports and what it does not.
Gaps: missing timestamps, deleted posts, and unverifiable reposts are explicit.
Confidence: tied to source custody. Uncertainty: attribution remains limited
when primary-source evidence is absent.

## Counterintelligence

Skill: `counterintelligence.denial_and_deception_detection`.

Evidence: planted-evidence indicators, source anomalies, and collection context
are separated. Inference: deception hypotheses are compared against benign
alternatives. Gaps: missing collection detail is not filled in. Confidence:
depends on converging indicators. Uncertainty: alternative explanations remain
open until discriminating observations arrive.

## Information Environment

Skill: `information_environment.narrative_ecosystem_mapping`.

Evidence: narratives, carrier types, amplification observations, and audience
segments are listed. Inference: ecosystem structure is described without
optimizing amplification. Gaps: private platform data and missing timelines are
named. Confidence: bounded by observable public signals. Uncertainty:
coordination claims stay tentative without corroboration.

## Research Methods

Skill: `research_methods.structured_literature_synthesis`.

Evidence: citations, methods, findings, conflicts, and limitations are tabulated.
Inference: synthesis separates direct study results from interpretation. Gaps:
missing literatures and unresolved conflicts are named. Confidence: strongest
where methods and findings converge. Uncertainty: alternative explanations and
source-quality limits remain visible.
